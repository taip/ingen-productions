InGen Productions is an ensemble of artistic engineers who utilize theatre, dance, and music to resurrect contemporary popular texts to the stage.

InGen focuses specifically on creating live performance experiences adapted from the developing new canon of work that has emerged in the video age. InGen sees film, television, and popular music as texts that reflect society, just as the classics do. InGen explores memory, recognition, collective experiences, contemporary myth, low budget spectacle, interdisciplinary dialogue, and the art of the remix.
